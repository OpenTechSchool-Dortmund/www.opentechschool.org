OpenTechSchool Org.

Hi, we are OpenTechSchool, a distributed community initiative organising welcoming and inclusive code learning happenings with real people in the real world. Our offers are for technology enthusiasts of all ages, genders, backgrounds, and experience levels. On top, we invite anyone to become an organiser themselves, set up their own local chapter organising learning events and join our community of coaches everywhere in creating original teaching material under the open Creative Commons Licence.

Learning

Eager to start learning how to code? Are you teaching yourself programming and feeling lost? Find out the next events that take place near you.
If there isn’t an OTS chapter in your city yet and you are willing to teach code to yourself, you can follow one of our tutorials online.
Stay connected with the OTS community! If you get stuck, you can always ask for support on our chat and stay update about future events.

Coaching

Want to give back to the tech community and meet other awesome technologists?
Check out our forum , find your favorite topic and just say “hello” there!
Didn’t find your favorite topic and strongly believe everybody should learn it?
Suggest it! Just open a new topic on our forum describing the topic you want to coach, your city and how you want to help.

Organizing

Want to learn something that isn’t offered? Organize a new workshop so that you can learn it! Start by reading our workshops guidelines.
Then get in touch with your local OTS or drop us an email.
No OTS in your city yet? Start one! Feel free to reach us via our forum or drop us an email to get all the help you need.
Also, if you are interested in joining our foundation as a member, learn more here .
